# E8 Trader Stage Objectives

You will get to the E8 Trader stage once you pass all the requirements of your evaluation. Users who follow the objectives and comply with user agreements are eligible to receive up to 100 % of any hypothetical earnings from our simulated platform and request payout.

**Trading Rules and Policies:**

**Unlimited Trading Days** ‚Äì There is no time limit, but you need to place and close at least one trade every 60 days.

**3% [Daily Drawdown](https://intercom.help/e8/en/articles/11769446-daily-drawdown)**‚Äì Maximum Floating or Closed loss calculated from your starting balance of the day.

**4%** [Dynamic](https://help.e8markets.com/en/articles/11782996-trailing-drawdown) **[Drawdown](https://intercom.help/e8/en/articles/11782996-trailing-drawdown)**‚Äì Maximum Running (floating) or Closed loss can not exceed the Dynamic drawdown loss level limit.

**Criteria for the payout:**

**40% [Best Day rule](https://intercom.help/e8/en/articles/10450125-40-best-day-rule)** \- With a 40% best-day rule, you must ensure that no single trading day exceeds more than 40% of your total generated profits.

Your net profit (payout share) must be greater than 50% of your daily drawdown.

Example: If you have a $100,000 account and your daily drawdown is 4%, the minimum amount required for a payout is $2,001.

Please note that at certain scenarios, [you should leave a buffer](https://help.e8markets.com/en/articles/9323884-payout-share-request-from-e8-trader-account-on-e8-one) when you are requesting a payout.

# News trading

In phase 1, you can trade news without any restrictions.

In an E8 Trader account during the release of high-impact news, a prohibition period will be enforced. This period will commence 5 minutes before the scheduled news release and extend for 5 minutes after the release.

During the prohibition period, users are prohibited from:

a. Opening new trades (Market Execution/Limit orders).

b. Closing trades (Market Execution/Limit orders).

c. Stop/Loss and Take/Profit

d. Buy Stop and Sell Stop

**Please note that if any of the prohibited actions are executed within the 10-minute period (5 Minutes before and 5 minutes after) immediately before or after the event, any profits earned during this time will be subject to removal, without any account violation.**

# Is there a consistency rule?

During the evaluation, there is no consistency rule, however, on the E8 Trader Stage, there is a 40% [Best Day rule](https://intercom.help/e8/en/articles/10450125-40-best-day-rule) \- With a 40% best-day rule, you must ensure that no single trading day exceeds more than 40% of your total generated profits.
